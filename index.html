About

Hello there! I'm Sree Parvathi, a 22-year-old individual driven by curiosity and a passion for innovation. Born on April 7th, 2002, I've had the privilege of experiencing a multicultural upbringing and education.

Having been brought up in the vibrant and diverse landscape of the United Arab Emirates, I traversed educational paths across borders, completing my secondary and higher education both in the UAE and India.
